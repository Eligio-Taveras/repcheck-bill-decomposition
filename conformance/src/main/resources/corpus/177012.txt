[Congressional Bills 104th Congress] [From the U.S. Government Publishing Office] [S. 2159 Engrossed in Senate (ES)] 2d Session S. 2159 _______________________________________________________________________ AN ACT To set the time for the convening of the One Hundred Fifth Congress.
